FBI Investigating Potentional Chemical Weapons Attack at Thomas Jefferson's Historic Home

TELL CITY - FBI Director Christoper Wray is asking for the whereabouts of suspected terrorist Elva Dickman. "When reports came in from Monticello of a foul stench hospitalizing visitors, its standard FBI procedure to try and rule out terrorism," Wray commented.
"Upon further investigation we found an obscene quantity of years-old stale urine, in amounts that suggest there was nearly an oil drum of it poured around the property. We also found threads in it that we traced back to a pair of early 2000s Lane Bryant XXXXL women's pants."


Wray is calling on the community to help track down Elva, who quickly matched the FBI's psychological profile of a suspect that: "Is a seemingly nice old woman, but completely lacks the ability to bake cakes of any ordinary, recognizable variety."

Elva's husband, local vegan gender rights activist Bob Dickman, refused to comment for our story until our paper: "publicly recognizes and agrees to use all 234 gender pronouns."
